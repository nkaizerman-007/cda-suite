About CDA

We know the media.
We know your audience.

CDA is Israel's first content discovery agency. We hold signed agreements with every major media outlet in the country — broadcast, digital, niche publishers, and everything in between.

What makes us different isn't the access. It's what we do with it. We start with your brief, not our inventory — your audience, your regulatory constraints, your competitive landscape, your budget reality. Then we find what actually fits.

Niche audience? Regulated industry? Saturated channel? We find what actually works.

Creative matches no one else would make

We don't just slot brands into obvious placements. We find unexpected combinations — the podcast your competitors haven't touched, the editorial angle that makes your message feel native, the channel that actually reaches your audience.

Access no one else has

Signed agreements with every major Israeli media body means first look at opportunities before they're widely available — and the leverage to negotiate terms that actually work for your budget.

Regulation and complexity are our specialty

Pharma, finance, legal — industries where other agencies slow down or say no, we've built deep expertise. If there's a compliant path, we know where it is.
